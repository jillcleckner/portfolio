Content Analysis

Conducted an analysis on existing content to uncover any gaps in topics. 

So many articles, no organization system...

Moon Marketing had a lot of content in its resivoir but there was no system in place to organize and track topics. With SEO shifting to a topic cluster model this was a good time to leverage this practice. 

Broken down into digestible pieces...

Utilizing HubSpot's content strategy tool I began creating topic clusters. I uncovered gaps in content and dated articles that were in need of updating. I created a schedule for updating republishing one article a week. Ensuring SEO and backlinks to increase domain authority and drive traffic to the website.
